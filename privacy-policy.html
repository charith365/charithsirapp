Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Welcome to Charith Sir ICT. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Data We Collect

  • Personal Identity: Full Name, NIC (for verification), Gender, and Date of Birth.
  • Contact Information: Email address and WhatsApp/Mobile phone numbers.
  • Academic Data: Exam year, Medium (Sinhala/English), and registered Institutes.
  • Device Data: We collect Device IDs and OS types to prevent account sharing and ensure security.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to maintain your academic progress, sync your video lessons across devices, and manage your access to premium ICT resources.

3. Third-Party Services

Our application is built using Google Firebase. Data is stored securely on Google Cloud servers. We use Firebase Authentication for secure logins and Firestore for real-time progress syncing.

4. Account & Data Deletion

In compliance with App Store and Play Store policies, you have the right to delete your account at any time. You can:

  • Go to the Profile Settings section within the app and select "Delete Account."
  • Contact us via email at charithsirapp@gmail.com to request manual deletion.

Upon request, all your personal data, including your video progress and profile image, will be permanently removed from our databases.

5. Security

While we use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) and Firebase Security Rules to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We strive to protect your personal information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for G.C.E. A/L and GIT students. If you are a parent and discover that your child under 13 has provided us with data without your consent, please contact us immediately.
