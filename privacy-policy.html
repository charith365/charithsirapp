Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

At Charith Sir ICT, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your personal data to provide an optimal learning experience.

1. Data We Collect

  • Identity Information: Name, NIC, Gender, and Date of Birth.
  • Contact Details: Email address and WhatsApp/Phone numbers.
  • Academic Data: Class year, Medium, and registered Institutes.
  • Device Information: Device IDs and OS types to ensure account security.
  • Payment Details: We store payment *status* as provided by physical institutes, but NO bank or card details are ever collected within the app.

2. Use of Your Data

Your information is used to sync your 13-Island Mission progress, track your video lesson progress, and verify your access to specific classes based on institute enrollment.

3. Data Infrastructure

We use Google Firebase for authentication and database management. Your data is stored securely on Google Cloud servers. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Account & Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data. To do so, you may:

  • Use the "Delete Account" feature in the App Profile settings.
  • Email charith365@gmail.com with your User ID.

Once requested, your profile image, progress, and personal details will be permanently removed from our servers within 7 days.

5. Security

We prioritize the security of your data but remind you that no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We utilize Firebase security rules and encryption to keep your information safe.
